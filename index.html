Frequently Asked Questions

How long will the project take?
All projects are unique and different. The timeline of completion is based on all the necessary steps that need to be taken in order to ensure the project is completed. We will always provide a detailed schedule for each step of the project.
What Michigan areas do you service?
Cornerstone Custom Brick services areas primarily in Wayne, Macomb, Oakland, Livingston, and Washtenaw counties.
Do you do repairs on brick fireplaces?
Yes. Cornerstone Custom Brick can beautify the look of your interior fireplace, and your exterior chimney by adhering cultured stone directly to brick or frame structures.
Can you match the style and pattern of my brick?
Yes. Cornerstone Custom Brick uses several local area brick suppliers. Only the best matches are selected for your project.
Will you remove the debris after you're done?
Yes. We understand that masonry projects can get dusty and cluttered. Rest assured that all residual debris from the project will be cleaned up and removed from the property upon completion.
Can you repair my old brick porch?
Yes. Your brick porch is one of the most important features of your home. Not only does it provide an inviting entrance, but it also adds aesthetic appeal to your property. The masonry experts at Cornerstone Custom Brick can repair cracks in the mortar and bricks, which will help prevent further damage.
Can you fix the gaps and cracks in my bricks?
Yes. Michigan weather take a toll on your mortar and bricks. Cornerstone Custom Brick can can restore and beautify the brick surfaces with Tuckpointing. We can replace the damaged mortar between bricks with new mortar, restore the structure, and make it more attractive. Cornerstone Custom Brick remains one of the tuckpointing companies with the best online reviews, offering services in Clinton Twp., Macomb, and Farmington Hills.
Will you be able to match my brick mortar?
Yes. We'll use a custom mortar with a mixture of pigments and tints that will blend to match. The replacing of damaged mortar within brick, concrete, and stone is called tuckpointing. This process restores a structure and prevents it from eventually collapsing.
Do you do brick paving repairs?
Yes. We are certified brick paver repair specialists and we are capable of repairing your pavers and making them look and feel as good as new!
Can you repair all the gaps in my driveway?
Yes. Cornerstone Custom Brick can solve your winter cracking blues using a polyurethane sealant. After being applied, the sealant allows for expansion and contraction between the saw cuts of your concrete walkway, patio, or driveway, with variations created by heat and cold. This preventative process will add longevity to your existing walkway, patio, or driveway.
